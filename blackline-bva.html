Methodology & Assumptions

This Business Value Assessment applies a conservative, benchmark-driven model to the value drivers BlackLine delivers across the Record-to-Report (RTR) lifecycle. Each quantified driver is calculated directly from your inputs using the basis noted on its card. Figures are directional estimates intended to frame a business case, not a guarantee of results.

  • Process Efficiency & Labor Optimization — recaptured F&A capacity: ~38% of close/recon effort (≈30% of total F&A time) automated, valued at fully-loaded cost.
  • Working-Capital Optimization — freed cash from a 5-day DSO reduction, valued at WACC; plus intercompany liquidity release.
  • Talent Retention & Empowerment — a 4-point turnover reduction × replacement cost (~75% of salary).
  • Regulatory Compliance — ~18% reduction in controls & audit cost, plus avoided misstatement/penalty risk.
  • M&A Integration — avoided integration FTEs and faster Day-1 readiness per deal.
  • Trust Premium — ~15 bps borrowing-cost improvement on outstanding debt from higher-quality, auditable financials.
  • Innovation Capacity, AI Readiness, Real-Time Decision Making — strategic enablers shown with directional value where applicable.
